About the Founder

Nihal VK (born 10-03-2003) is a multi-disciplinary innovator and tech entrepreneur from Kerala, India. As a passionate Computer Engineering student (2022–2025) at Madin College of Engineering and Management, he’s committed to reshaping how humans interact with machines.

He is the Founder & CEO of Futurinx and Cerebra Connect (a platform focused on BCI-powered communication), and has also co-founded:

  • AppTech Gig – A collaborative software solutions venture (2024–25)
  • Synapse Spice Lab – Research lab focusing on brain-inspired computing and circuits (2024–present)
  • Chief Creative Officer at MCEM (2023–25)

Nihal’s vision is to bridge futuristic ideas with practical technology through platforms like Futurinx.
